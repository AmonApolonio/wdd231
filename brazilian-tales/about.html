About Brazilian Folklore Tales

Hi there! My name is Amon Apolonio, and I've always been fascinated by Brazilian folklore. I think there’s not enough information about it online, so I decided to create this site to help people discover and appreciate these amazing stories and their importance to Brazil’s culture.

Brazilian folklore is full of amazing myths, legends, and traditions that have been shared from generation to generation. From the playful Saci Pererê to the magical Iara, these stories show the mix of cultures that make Brazil so unique. I hope this site makes you curious to learn more and enjoy these tales as much as I do.

I had so much fun making this site, and I want to keep improving it. One of my future plans is to add fun activities to introduce folklore to kids in an engaging way. These activities will include:

  • Interactive Stories
  • Fun Puzzles
  • Coloring Activities

By making folklore more interactive and accessible, I hope to spark curiosity and creativity in young minds while preserving these timeless tales.

All the illustrations on this site were created by me using Photoshop and a tool called Invoke. If you're interested in seeing more folklore-inspired illustrations, feel free to check out the Instagram account I created: https://www.instagram.com/folklore_dive.
